Initial Research

Test

BOSSTEP's research domain was within children's health.Our groups goal was to contribute to children health through increasing physical activity time and raising awareness to parents and kids on what is healthy for children.

The Idea

Test

From our research we learned phsyical activity was needed and 93% of 6-12 year olds had access to cellphones. This sprouted the idea of creating a RPG game with a focus on exercise.

Wireframes

Test

With our goal being to support childhood health, we wanted to create an exercising app that would appeal to kids. Thus, we decided on the inclusion of game-centric features to attract and interest children. We settled with the main feature of our app revolving on step tracking as a form of excercise that would not be too grueling for kids. We decided to go with step tracking as our main feature as it provided a form of excerise that was simple and easy for children. Furthermore, simply tracking their steps would mean children would not be distracted by having to constantly looking at their phones while playing.
