Buddies

In order to keep children more invested into the app, they are able to collect multiple different buddies to accompany them in the game. When first starting the game they are able to choose one out of three buddies as their initial partner, and are able to unlock more as they progress through the game.

Achievements

To keep children interested in the game, and thus keep them active, we chose to tie together achievements with buddies. Specific achievements are tied to specific buddies, so unlocking an achievement will unlock the corresponding buddy. While there are multiple different achievements to be unlocked, they all require some sort of physical activity to achieve.

Friends and Party

Social features such as friends and party are added in order to further entice kids to keep playing. By scanning a QR code, kids are able to befriend each other and create parties. Playing in party mode allows kids to journey and fight bosses together. However, as our goal is to have kids moving as much as possible, playing in party mode will scale the required steps depending on the amount of party members.
