Research

Findings

Consolidated the feedback from our interviews, we found that our intended user demographic includes two main groups. The primary group includes tech savvy young adults who are interested in, or already own a mechanical keyboard. The second group is catered towards novice keyboard enthusiasts looking to take on their first custom keyboard build or who want to purchase a prebuilt board.
